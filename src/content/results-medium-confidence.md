# Medium Confidence Legal Discrepancies

> Dose pairings where the matched products share the same active ingredient but may differ slightly in strength (confidence = MEDIUM). This is the broader net — it captures more substances but includes less-exact matches.

### Why Mixed Direction Occurs

A substance appears in both directions when the same active ingredient has products at different legal statuses within a single country. For example, **Clotrimazole**: the UK has it as GSL (Canesten cream at low strength), P (pharmacy pessaries), and POM (higher strengths), while the US has it as both OTC and RX. Depending on which specific product is matched, the discrepancy can go either way.
