> Dose pairings where the matched products have identical ingredients and identical strength (confidence = HIGH), but one country requires a prescription and the other does not.

## Summary

- **44** dose pairings where the **UK is more accessible** (P or GSL) than the US (RX)
- **24** dose pairings where the **US is more accessible** (OTC) than the UK (POM)
